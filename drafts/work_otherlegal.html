Extraprofessional Legal Experience

Outside of the day job, I take great pride in being able to use my legal knowledge and training to assist a range of people around me with a variety of different issues on a friendly basis. Some examples of my recent work include

  • Employment Disputes - assisting a friend following an unfriendly dismissal to reclaim unpaid wages and improper deductions.
  • Commercial Contracts Advice - assisting friends with a new venture by preparing standard client terms and conditions.
  • Financial Services Dispute - assisted a family member to recover from their financial service providers for missold PPI
  • Data Protection and Privacy Advice - advising a local charity on its data protection policy and website privacy terms.
  • Property Disputes - representing a family member in a Deposit Protection Service dispute over wrongful deductions from a long term lease.

I'm always keen to help where I can, so if you have an issue that you think I might be able to help with, please do get in touch here
